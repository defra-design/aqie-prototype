Air quality management areas (AQMAs)

If a local authority finds any places where the national air quality objectives are not likely to be achieved, it must decalre an Air Quality Manage Area. This area could be just one or two streets, or it could be much bigger.

Smoke Control Areas

A smoke control area is an area where people and businesses must not:

  • emit a substantial amount of smoke from a chimney
  • buy or sell authorised fuel for use in a smoke control area unless it is used in an ‘exempt applicance’ (appliances which are approved for use in smoke control areas)
